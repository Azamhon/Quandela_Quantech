03 · DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

EVERY DECISION IS
A LOGICAL CONSEQUENCE

AUTOENCODERWhy not PCA?
  • →PCA is linear — swaptions are nonlinear
  • →Denoising AE = nonlinear manifold + imputation
  • →L1 sparsity → each dim = distinct market factor
RESERVOIRWhy not VQC?
  • →494 samples — circuits would overfit instantly
  • →Fixed reservoirs → zero barren plateaus
  • →Random expansion + trained readout = optimal
PREDICTIONWhy latent space?
  • →20-dim ≪ 224-dim — far easier optimization
  • →AE decoder constrains outputs to manifold
ENSEMBLEWhy 3 reservoirs?
  • →Different photon counts = different Fock regions
  • →Cubic + quartic + pairwise = physics diversity
